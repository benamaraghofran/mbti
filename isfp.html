ISFP
Adventurer
aka Composer, Producer,The Artist
Role: Explorer / Artisan
"I change during the course of a day. I wake and I'm one person, and when I go to sleep I know for certain I'm somebody else." — Bob Dylan
Flexible and charming artists, always ready to explore and experience something new. Adventurers live in a colorful, sensual world, inspired by connections with people and ideas. They push the limits of social convention.

STRENGTHS:

  • Charming
  • Sensitive to Others
  • Imaginative,Passionate
  • Curious,Artistic

WEAKNESSES:

  • Fiercely Independent
  • Unpredictable,Easily tressed
  • Overly Competitive
  • Fluctuating Self-Esteem

CAREERS:

Adventurers are natural artists, musicians and photographers, as well as designers of all stripes.
