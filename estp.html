ESTP
Entrepreneur
aka Promoter, Executor, The Doer
Role: Explorer / Artisan
"Life is either a daring adventure or nothing at all." – Helen Keller
Smart, energetic and very perceptive people who truly enjoy living on the edge. Sometimes Entrepreneurs' instantaneous observation and action is just what's required, as in some corporate environments, and especially in emergencies.

STRENGTHS:

  • Bold, Rational
  • Practical, Original
  • Perceptive, Direct
  • Sociable

WEAKNESSES:

  • Insensitive, Impatient,
  • Risk-prone, Unstructured
  • May Miss the Bigger Picture
  • Defiant

CAREERS:

Sales, business negotiations, marketing, and acting are a great fit for ESTPs' boldness and improvisational skills.

FAMOUS ESTPs:

Ernest Hemingway, Jack Nicholson, Madonna, Bruce Willis, Michael J. Fox, Nicolas Sarkozy, Samuel L. Jackson
