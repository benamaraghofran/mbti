ESFP
Entertainer
aka Motivator, Presenter,The Performer
Role: Explorer / Artisan
"We Are All of Us Stars... And We Deserve to Twinkle"
Spontaneous, energetic and enthusiastic people – life is never boring around them. Entertainers get caught up in the excitement of the moment, and want everyone else to feel that way, too. No other type is as generous with their time and energy when encouraging others.

STRENGTHS:

  • Bold, Original
  • Aesthetics, Showmanship
  • Practical, Observant
  • Excellent People Skills

WEAKNESSES:

  • Sensitive, Conflict-Averse
  • Poor Long-Term Planners
  • Easily Bored
  • Unfocused

CAREERS:

Natural event planners, sales representatives, trip planners. Also counselors, social workers, personal coaches, consultants.

FAMOUS ESFPs:

Elton John, Marilyn Monroe, Jamie Oliver, Adele, Jamie Foxx, Steve Irwin, Miley Cyrus
