ESTJ
Executive
aka Implementor,The Supervisor
Role: Sentinel / Guardian
“Good order is the foundation of all things.” — Edmund Burke
Excellent administrators, unsurpassed at managing things—or people. Executives are classic images of the model citizen: they help their neighbors, uphold the law, and try to make sure that everyone participates in the communities and organizations they hold so dear.

STRENGTHS:

  • Dedicated, Strong-willed
  • Direct, Honest, Loyal,Patient, Reliable
  • Enjoy Creating Order
  • Excellent Organizers

WEAKNESSES:

  • Inflexible, Stubborn
  • Judgmental, Too Focused on Social Status
  • Difficult to Relax
  • Difficulty Expressing Emotion

CAREERS:

Well-respected organizations, law enforcement, military service, hospitals, and prominent legal firms.

FAMOUS ESTJs:

Sonia Sotomayor, John D. Rockefeller, Judge Judy, Ella Baker, Frank Sinatra, James Monroe, Laura Linney
