ESFJ
Consul
aka Facilitator, Caretaker,The Caregiver
Role: Sentinel / Guardian
“Encourage, lift and strengthen one another. For the positive energy spread to one will be felt by us all.” — Deborah Day
Extraordinarily caring, social, and popular people, always eager to help. Consuls are altruists, and they take seriously their responsibility to help and to do the right thing. ESFJs love to be of service and feel valued.

STRENGTHS:

  • Strong Practical Skills
  • Strong Sense of Duty
  • Very Loyal, Sensitive
  • Warm, Good at Connecting with Others

WEAKNESSES:

  • Worried about Their Social Status,Inflexible
  • Reluctant to Innovate or Improvise
  • Vulnerable to Criticism
  • Often Too Needy,Too Selfless

CAREERS:

Religious work or counseling. Careers as administrators are a natural fit, and they make surprisingly good accountants.

FAMOUS ESFJs:

Taylor Swift, Jennifer Garner, Bill Clinton, Steve Harvey, Danny Glover, Jennifer Lopez, Tyra Banks
