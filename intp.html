INTP
Logician
aka Designer, Theoriser, The Scientist
Role: Analyst / Rational
“Learn from yesterday, live for today, hope for tomorrow. The important thing is not to stop questioning.” – Albert Einstein
Innovative inventors with an unquenchable thirst for knowledge. Logicians are known for their brilliant theories and unrelenting logic – they are considered the most logically precise of all the personality types.

STRENGTHS:

  • Great Analysts and Abstract
  • Thinkers, Imaginative
  • Original, Open-Minded
  • Enthusiastic, Objective
  • Honest and Straightforward

WEAKNESSES:

  • Very Private and Withdrawn
  • Insensitive, Absent-minded
  • Condescending, Loathe Rules and Guidelines
  • Second-Guess Themselves

CAREERS:

Mathematicians, systems analysts, and career scientists, especially in more abstract fields such as physics.

FAMOUS INTPs:

Bill Gates, Kristen Stewart, Albert Einstein, Stanley Crouch, Isaac Newton, René Descartes, Ellen Page
