Don't Lose That 'Little Spark of Madness'. You Can Change the World With Just an Idea
Enthusiastic, creative and sociable free spirits, who can always find a reason to smile. ENFPs are fiercely independent, and more than stability and security, they crave creativity and freedom. They see life as a big , complex puzzle where everything is connected.

STRENGTHS:

  • Curious, Observant
  • Energetic, Enthusiastic
  • Excellent Communicators, Know How to Relax
  • Very Popular and Friendly

WEAKNESSES:

  • Poor Practical Skills, Find it
  • Difficult to Focus, Overthink Things
  • Get Stressed Easily, Highly Emotional
  • Independent to a Fault

CAREERS:

Any human science or service, like psychology, counseling, teaching, politics, diplomacy or detective work.

FAMOUS ENFPs:

Robert Downey Jr., Robin Williams, Quentin Tarantino, Kelly Clarkson, Will Smith, Ellen DeGeneres
